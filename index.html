About the game.

Plague Doctor is a strategic game that involves the player having to control the spread of a disease. It is set in a dystopian future where society has collapsed due to global warming and regressed into a pre-industrial state.

Game Story

Bleak Future

  • Human civilisation as we know it has collapsed. The greed and desires of Man have all but ruined the fragile ecosystems of the Earth. Industrial scale activities have polluted the atmosphere and caused the Earth’s seas to flood vast swathes of land.

Death & Disease

  • Uncontrolled outbreaks of diseases are common and widespread. Billions have perished and so will what little remains of Humanity. Mysterious doctors have been sent far and wide to every human settlement in order to cure and contain those unlucky enough to still be alive.

Plan & Strategy

  • You have been sent to a remote village suffering from outbreaks of deadly diseases. Plan your every move and ensure you stay one step ahead of the disease who’s only purpose is to kill. Not everyone can be saved, so be prepared to make the ultimate sacrifice.
